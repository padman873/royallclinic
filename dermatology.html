When To Visit

Persistent skin problems deserve proper review

A skin condition that keeps returning, spreading, bleeding, hurting, or disrupting sleep should be assessed rather than repeatedly treated blindly.

Useful reasons to book

  • Rashes that are worsening, painful, swollen, or spreading.
  • Acne that is leaving marks or not improving with skincare.
  • Itch, flaking, or eczema that keeps recurring.
  • Suspected fungal infection, wart, cyst, or skin growth.
  • Dark patches or pigmentation that need diagnosis before treatment.
