What We Screen

Infertility assessment for both partners

Royall Clinic reviews medical history, hormone health, menstrual cycles, sperm health and possible factors that may affect your ability to conceive.

Female fertility review

Evaluation of menstrual pattern, hormone tests, pelvic pain, ovulation issues and reproductive history.

Male fertility review

Assessments can include sperm health, hormone balance, and any medical factors affecting male reproductive function.

Couple-focused care

We look at both partners together to understand the full picture before recommending tests or referrals.

Screening Details

What to expect in your visit

The clinic offers a sensitive consultation that includes medical review, test recommendations, and personalised advice tailored to your fertility goals.

Common checks

  • Medical history and reproductive timeline review.
  • Hormone testing including thyroid, prolactin, and ovarian reserve.
  • Blood tests and ultrasound referral where clinically indicated.
  • Advice on lifestyle, nutrition, and next steps for fertility support.
