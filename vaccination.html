Before Vaccination

A quick safety review comes first

Some vaccines need timing adjustments or extra caution depending on illness, allergies, pregnancy, immune status, or medication.

Tell the doctor if you have

  • Fever, current infection, or feeling acutely unwell.
  • Previous vaccine allergy or severe allergic reaction.
  • Pregnancy, breastfeeding, or trying to conceive.
  • Immune system conditions or immune-suppressing medication.
  • Upcoming travel, surgery, or workplace vaccination deadline.
