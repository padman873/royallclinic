Long-Term Control

The goal is fewer flares and better skin comfort

Many skin diseases cannot be solved with a single cream. The right plan may include medication, trigger avoidance, skincare changes, and review.

Book a review if you notice

  • Rashes lasting more than two weeks or repeatedly returning.
  • Severe itch, pain, scaling, cracking, or bleeding.
  • Skin symptoms affecting sleep, work, school, or confidence.
  • Marks, scars, pigmentation, or thickened skin after inflammation.
  • Use of repeated steroid creams without a clear diagnosis.
