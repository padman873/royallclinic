Treatment Focus

Facial concerns we commonly manage

Royall Clinic combines clinical assessment with aesthetic judgement, selecting treatment pathways based on your skin condition, downtime tolerance, and desired level of correction.

Pigmentation & Uneven Tone

Doctor-guided plans for melasma, sun spots, post-inflammatory marks, and patchy facial pigmentation.

Acne & Scarring

Support for active breakouts, clogged pores, post-acne marks, and textural scarring using clinical skin protocols.

Skin Rejuvenation

Programmes for dullness, fine lines, dehydration, enlarged pores, and skin quality maintenance.

Facial Lifting

Non-surgical tightening and contour support for mild laxity, lower-face heaviness, and softened definition.

Texture Refinement

Targeted care for roughness, visible pores, acne texture, and uneven surface quality.

Maintenance Plans

Long-term skin health routines designed around your lifestyle, budget, and response to treatment.
