When It Is Urgent

Some wounds should not wait

Deep, heavily bleeding, contaminated, facial, bite-related, or function-affecting injuries may need emergency or specialist care.

Seek urgent help if there is

  • Bleeding that does not stop with firm pressure.
  • Numbness, weakness, or difficulty moving the area.
  • Animal or human bite, rusty object, or dirty wound.
  • Deep cut on the face, hand, joint, genital area, or near the eye.
  • Fever, spreading redness, pus, or worsening pain after injury.
