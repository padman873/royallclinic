When To Test

Timing matters for accurate results

Testing too early can produce unclear or false results. Royall Clinic reviews your cycle, symptoms, and timing to recommend the best approach.

Consider testing if you have

  • A missed period or irregular menstrual cycle.
  • Positive pregnancy symptoms such as nausea, breast tenderness, or fatigue.
  • Recent unprotected sex with pregnancy concern.
  • A previous home test result you want confirmed clinically.
  • A need for early reproductive health advice.
