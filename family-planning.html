Private Guidance

A safe space for practical questions

Family planning is personal. The aim is to help you choose safely and confidently, without pressure.

Useful topics to discuss

  • Which contraceptive method fits your health and lifestyle.
  • Possible side effects and warning signs to watch for.
  • Planning contraception after delivery or while breastfeeding.
  • Irregular bleeding after starting or stopping contraception.
  • When to do a pregnancy test or arrange follow-up.
