ABOUT US

Royall Clinic

Royall Clinic was established in 2018 and is proudly owned by Novaxel Sdn Bhd.

Originally founded as a dedicated general practice clinic, Royall Clinic has since evolved into a premier doctor-led primary care and aesthetic center. Today, we seamlessly integrate comprehensive general medicine with advanced, evidence-based aesthetic treatments.

Located at Pusat Perniagaan Bestari in Bandar Bestari, Klang, Selangor, our clinic is led by highly experienced medical professionals. We combine modern medical expertise with personalized care, focusing on preventive medicine and wellness. At Royall Clinic, we are dedicated to helping our patients achieve optimal health, renewed confidence, and an enhanced quality of life.
