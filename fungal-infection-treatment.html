Common Fungal Presentations

Fungal infections of skin, nails and scalp

Fungal infections may appear as itchy rashes, flaky patches, nail discoloration, or scalp scaling. A clinical review is important to identify the correct cause and treatment.

Skin fungus

Includes ringworm, athlete's foot, jock itch, and other fungal rashes that need medical therapy.

Nail fungus

Toenail or fingernail fungal infections often require longer medical treatment and monitoring for full clearance.

Scalp fungus

Scalp fungal infections can cause flaking, redness, itch, and hair changes, with targeted treatment to calm the scalp.

Diagnosis & Treatment

Accurate diagnosis leads to better outcomes

The clinic assesses symptoms, appearance, and history, then recommends medication, creams, or further testing if needed to resolve the infection.

Our approach

  • Clinical evaluation of the affected area and symptoms.
  • Prescription topical or oral antifungal medication when needed.
  • Nail care advice and follow-up review for persistent cases.
  • Guidance on hygiene and skin care to prevent recurrence.
