Everyday medical care

General treatment for acute and ongoing health needs

From coughs and fevers to chronic condition checks, our doctors provide the medical guidance, prescriptions, and follow-up needed for safe management.

Acute symptom review

Rapid assessment for fever, cough, sore throat, abdominal pain, headache, and other urgent symptoms.

Infection management

Clinical evaluation for respiratory, urinary, skin, and soft tissue infections with evidence-based treatment.

Chronic disease support

Doctor-led monitoring for diabetes, hypertension, cholesterol, asthma, and long-term medical conditions.

Medication review

Prescription review and adjustment to keep medicines safe, effective, and aligned with your current health needs.

Preventive advice

Medical guidance on vaccination, screening, lifestyle changes, and early care to avoid escalation.

Referral coordination

When specialist care is needed, we help coordinate the next step with appropriate referrals and documentation.

See a doctor when

Early assessment improves comfort and outcomes

General treatment helps you avoid delays. Seeing a doctor early can reduce complications and keep your care plan on track.

Book a review if you have

  • Persistent fever, chills, pain, or infection symptoms.
  • Shortness of breath, chest discomfort, or palpitations.
  • Worsening diabetes, blood pressure, or other chronic conditions.
  • Medication concerns, side effects, or unclear instructions.
  • Sudden changes in appetite, energy, digestion, or urinary symptoms.

How general treatment works

01

Assess

02

Diagnose

03

Treat

Appropriate medicines, referrals, or follow-up plans are provided.

04

Review

Care is tracked until the issue stabilises or specialist care is arranged.
