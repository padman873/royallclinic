What It Checks

Fast clues from a simple sample

A urine test can support diagnosis and screening, but results are best interpreted with symptoms, examination, medical history, and other tests where needed.

UTI Symptoms

Testing may help assess burning urination, frequency, urgency, lower abdominal discomfort, or cloudy urine.

Blood Or Protein

Urinalysis can detect blood or protein traces that may need repeat testing or further doctor review.

Sugar & Ketones

Urine screening may show sugar or ketones, which can support diabetes or metabolic assessment.

Pregnancy-Related Checks

Urine testing can be part of pregnancy, antenatal, or symptom-based review depending on the concern.

Workplace Screening

Selected urine checks may support medical checkups, health screening, or administrative requirements.

Doctor Review

Your doctor explains what the result may mean and whether medication, culture, blood tests, or imaging is needed.

When To Test

Do not ignore urinary symptoms

Early review helps reduce complications, especially for children, pregnant patients, elderly patients, men, and people with diabetes or kidney problems.

Book a review if you have

  • Pain or burning when passing urine.
  • Frequent urination, urgency, or lower abdominal pain.
  • Blood in urine, fever, back pain, or vomiting.
  • Recurring urinary symptoms or previous kidney issues.
  • Abnormal urine results from a screening package.
