When To Treat

Early intervention delivers better outcomes

Hair loss progresses over time, making early consultation and treatment critical. Royall Clinic assesses your condition and recommends the optimal combination of treatments for your unique situation.

Consider treatment if you experience

  • Progressive hair loss or receding hairline affecting confidence.
  • Thinning crown or pattern baldness limiting style options.
  • Scalp issues like dandruff, itching, or inflammation.
  • Interest in permanent restoration for natural-looking fullness.
  • Desire to enhance existing hair density and strength.
