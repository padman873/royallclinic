When To Screen

Testing can be useful even without symptoms

Some infections can be silent. Screening helps protect your health and reduces the risk of passing infection to others.

Consider screening if you have

  • Unprotected sex or a new sexual partner.
  • A partner who tested positive or has symptoms.
  • Genital sores, discharge, pain, itching, or urinary symptoms.
  • Previous STI and need follow-up or repeat testing.
  • Concern about exposure timing or window periods.
