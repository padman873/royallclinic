Recognise A Flare

Knowing when to see a doctor makes a difference

Many people treat symptoms at home until they return. A doctor review can separate seborrheic dermatitis from psoriasis, eczema, fungal irritation, or allergic dermatitis.

Book a review if you have

  • Persistent scalp flaking, oiliness, or itch despite dandruff shampoos.
  • Red, greasy patches on the face, eyebrows, or around the nose and ears.
  • Scalp soreness, hair shedding, or visible scaling in skin folds.
  • Recurring symptoms that improve then return after stopping treatment.
  • Difficulty choosing the right shampoo, cream, or medicated wash.
