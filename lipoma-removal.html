Benign Fatty Lumps

Professional removal with clinical care

Lipomas are benign fatty growths that are safe to remove. Royall Clinic offers sterile excision under local anaesthetic with careful wound management.

Assessment First

The doctor checks the lipoma, surrounding tissue, location, and whether removal in clinic is suitable.

Local Anaesthesia

Removal is performed comfortably with local anaesthetic to keep the area numb during the procedure.

Complete Excision

The lipoma is carefully removed in its entirety to reduce recurrence and allow proper healing.

Wound Care

After removal, the wound is closed with stitches and dressed to support optimal healing.

Follow-Up

Review is available to check healing, remove sutures when ready, and answer any recovery questions.

Infection Prevention

Doctor-led care aims to minimise infection risk and manage the surgical site safely.

When To Remove

Consider removal if the lipoma causes concern or discomfort

Lipomas are harmless fatty growths, but removal is recommended when they enlarge, become uncomfortable, or affect cosmetic appearance.

Book a review if you have

  • A growing or enlarging fatty lump under the skin.
  • Discomfort, pain, or pressure from the lipoma.
  • A lipoma in a visible or sensitive area causing cosmetic concern.
  • Difficulty moving or functioning due to the lump's location.
  • A lipoma that keeps returning or has been drained previously.
